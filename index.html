My Personal Interests

I enjoy collecting, and repairing, retro game consoles. Currently I focus on Nintendo consoles, the only console I am missing from that line up is the Virtual Boy. I also collect games that I either played growing up or I heard about from friends, but never had the chance to play. One of my favorite games to have re-discovered is Diddy Kong Racing on the Nintendo 64.

I also like to play around with game development in my free time. I like to try creating different game mechanics and experimenting with how to implement said mechanics. Some mechanics are specific ones I pull from existing games, some are common to a variety of games, and others I come up with in spurts of creativity. Not every game mechanic turns out, but I always end up enjoying the creative exercise it gives, and learning new skills along the way.

Another interest of mine is 3D Printing and 3D Modeling. I've dabbled in 3D modeling for a few years and have recently taken up 3D printing. Mostly I print things designed by others, however I've just started working on making my own models for printing. Its taking a lot of trial and error, but I am enjoying the process.
